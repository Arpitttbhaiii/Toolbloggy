How it works

Bond pricing calculation uses the Present Value (PV) formula. It discounts each future coupon payment and the final face value back to today's terms using the Yield to Maturity (YTM) as the discount rate.

If the YTM is higher than the Coupon Rate, the bond sells at a Discount. If the YTM is lower, it sells at a Premium. This tool assumes annual coupon payments.
