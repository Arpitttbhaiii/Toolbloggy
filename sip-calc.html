Understanding SIP Compounding

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) leverages the mathematical power of compounding. By investing a fixed amount regularly, you benefit from Rupee Cost Averaging, which reduces the impact of market volatility.

The formula used for this calculation is:
FV = P × [ (1 + r)^n - 1 ] / r × (1 + r)
